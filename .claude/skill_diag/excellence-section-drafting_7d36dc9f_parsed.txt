      "content": "MAESTRO (Multi-Agent Ecosystem for Strategic Task Reasoning and Orchestration) addresses a defining limitation of current AI agent architectures: the inability of large language model-based agents to sustain robust, coherent behaviour across long-horizon tasks in safety-critical and high-value real-world sectors. Current LLM-based agents degrade unpredictably over extended task sequences, cannot maintain coherent state across context-window boundaries, lack principled mechanisms for multi-agent coordination, and treat external tool and API integration as an afterthought rather than a core architectural requirement. These limitations prevent the deployment of truly autonomous AI systems in high-value sectors of the European economy.\n\nMAESTRO resolves these limitations through three mutually reinforcing research pillars \u2014 neuro-symbolic planning and reasoning, adaptive memory architecture, and decentralised multi-agent coordination \u2014 unified by a cross-cutting External Tool and API Orchestration Layer, co-engineered from the outset rather than assembled from independent research components. The project's central thesis is that these capabilities and their tool-integration substrate must be co-designed to produce agents that are robust, trustworthy, and deployable in real-world settings.
